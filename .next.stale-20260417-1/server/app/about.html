ABOUT STRXEET

Our Mission

FASHION THAT BELONGS ON THE STREETS

STRXEET was born in 2020 from a simple belief: Indian streetwear deserved to be world-class. We started in a garage in Bengaluru with three graphic tees and a vision. Today we're a full collection of contemporary urban clothing — designed, crafted, and shipped from India to the world.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Quality First

We spend months sourcing the right fabrics, testing fits, and perfecting every detail before a product ever ships.

Made in India

Every piece is designed and manufactured in India by skilled artisans. We're proud of our roots.

Sustainable Future

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint with responsible sourcing and minimal packaging.
